About Me

I'm a backend architect and founding engineer based in Bangalore with over 9 years of experience designing and building distributed systems that work at scale. I've owned entire system lifecycles — from architecture and API design to deployment and production operations — across enterprise SaaS platforms and zero-to-one product builds.

My core strengths lie in microservices architecture, real-time infrastructure, security design, and database systems. I've built platforms serving global automotive brands like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Toyota, and Subaru, and most recently co-founded and architected a social platform from the ground up in Go.

I combine deep architectural thinking with hands-on execution — I'm equally comfortable designing system architecture, shipping production code, and leading engineering teams. I care about writing clean, secure, and maintainable software that solves real problems.
